# Implementation Consultant

Vecticum develops software for the best employee experience with everyday tasks at work. Is it vacation applications, submitting invoices for approval, tracking budgets, managing trainings and business trips, updating employee and org. structure information, etc.

Decades of experience, know-how, and technology mix determined the product success which served for more than 200 companies over the world and growing x3 every year.

<h2>As an Implementation consultant you will:</h2>

- Provide product expertise and guidance to clients throughout the consulting and implementation
- Pro-actively develop and share best practices internally with Vecticum team and externally with clients
- Provide training and end-user support, during and/or after the implementation
- Provide manuals and system configuration documentation

<h2>As a part of Vecticum team you will:</h2>

- Be accountable for solution design
- Be accountable for the quality of configuration produced
- Be responsible for consultants adhering to system design standards
- Provide support to the development team to ensure system quality, functionality, and performance
- Be escalation point for technical issues
- Contribute to product backlog creation and sprint planning
- Contribute to project test strategies and provide oversight for test case production
- Support for sales opportunities as required

<h2>The company offers</h2>

- Self-education time and trainings from both, technology, and business matters
- Flexible work time, at Vecticum we strive to be an organization empower employees to be judged by their output not their hours. We measurable objectives, professional growth, and personal responsibility rather than rigid leave patterns
- Any specific need for gadgets or software
- Team building budget

<h2>Requirements:</h2>

- Significant relevant experience, gained within an implementation focused role and a software solutions organization
- Good understanding of business management processes in finance, supply chain management and/or personnel management
- Programming language - no development experience and knowledge are required, however understanding the JavaScript syntax, code reviewing and debugging experience is highly appreciated
- Any BSc degree, Computer Science, Physics, Economics, Business Management or Engineering
- Consultative, technical and problem-solving approach and experience
- Full time job

<h2>NICE TO HAVE</h2>

Vecticum software elements are highly configurable; so the good technical skills understanding configuration management patterns would be beneficial.

A typical day would involve a variety of activities, involving the whole lifecycle of a project: starting from customer requirements evaluation and solution preparation, solution configuration, preparing the development specification, testing functionality, providing consultancy support to the clients.
